# Manager Tier Hiring Guidelines

## 1. Purpose

These Guidelines govern selection of personnel for Imperial Manager tier
positions across all Imperial Service departments. They supplement the
public Imperial Recruitment Policy (v2.1, 2022) and are restricted to
Manager-tier personnel and above involved in hiring decisions.

## 2. Eligibility Standards

Manager tier candidates must satisfy all eligibility criteria in the
public Recruitment Policy, plus:

- **Service experience:** minimum 6 standard years of Imperial Service or
  equivalent verified service in an approved jurisdiction
- **Tier 1 background:** confirmed within the last 24 standard months
- **Restricted clearance:** held or eligible at the time of offer
- **Performance record:** no Marginal or Unsatisfactory rating in the
  prior 5 standard years
- **Disciplinary record:** no open disciplinary matters and no resolved
  matters within the prior 24 standard months involving moral turpitude,
  financial impropriety, or breach of classification

## 3. Sourcing Channels

Manager tier sourcing relies primarily on internal promotion. External
sourcing is permitted only with documented justification from the
sponsoring Director.

| Source | Target share |
|---|---|
| Internal promotion | 75% |
| Lateral transfer from allied jurisdictions | 15% |
| External direct hire | 10% (Director-approved only) |

The Office of Personnel Affairs reviews source-share compliance annually
and reports deviations to the relevant Executive.

## 4. Assessment Components

Manager tier assessment consists of:

1. **Performance file review.** Conducted by the sponsoring Director.
2. **Structured panel interview.** Three panelists at Director tier or
   above; one must be from a department other than the hiring department.
3. **Situational judgment exercise.** Two-hour scenario-based exercise
   covering personnel conflict resolution, resource allocation, and a
   classification-handling decision.
4. **Subordinate feedback.** Anonymized feedback from at least three
   personnel who reported to the candidate in their prior role. Required
   for internal promotion only.
5. **Background re-screen.** Updated Tier 2 screening including financial
   review and external association inventory.

Total elapsed time from candidate identification to offer averages 42
standard days.

## 5. Compensation at Tier Entry

Entry into the Imperial Manager tier salary band:

- **Internal promotion:** placed at Year 1–2 column, upper third of band
  (62,000 credits as of fiscal 7901)
- **Lateral transfer:** placement within band determined by prior service
  credit and equivalency review
- **External direct hire:** must be approved by the sponsoring Director;
  no entry above Year 3–5 column without Executive concurrence

A 12,000-credit signing supplement applies to external hires only and
vests over 18 standard months of continuous service.

## 6. Required Approvals

| Source | Approvals required |
|---|---|
| Internal promotion (same department) | Sponsoring Director |
| Internal promotion (cross department) | Sponsoring Director + receiving Director |
| Lateral transfer | Sponsoring Director + Office of Personnel Affairs |
| External direct hire | Sponsoring Director + Executive of receiving department |

## 7. Confidentiality

These Guidelines, panel discussions, candidate evaluations, and the
existence or absence of an active manager-tier search are Restricted.
Disclosure to non-cleared personnel — including candidates — is
prohibited.
