# Compensation and Benefits Handbook

## 1. Purpose

This Handbook details the standard compensation, benefits, and allowances
provided to Imperial personnel. It is published annually by the Office of
Compensation in coordination with the Office of Personnel Affairs and
supersedes all prior versions on the effective date listed above.

## 2. Salary Bands by Rank Tier

All figures are stated in credits per standard year. Bands apply to base
salary only and exclude hazard pay, station differentials, and one-time
bonuses.

| Rank tier | Years 1–2 | Years 3–5 | Years 6–10 | Years 11+ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Imperial Employee (T1) | 28,000 – 34,000 | 31,000 – 38,000 | 36,000 – 44,000 | 40,000 – 48,000 |
| Imperial Manager (T2) | 56,000 – 68,000 | 64,000 – 78,000 | 74,000 – 92,000 | 84,000 – 104,000 |
| Imperial Director (T3) | 118,000 – 142,000 | 132,000 – 168,000 | 154,000 – 198,000 | 178,000 – 224,000 |
| Imperial Executive (T4) | 248,000 – 312,000 | 286,000 – 364,000 | 332,000 – 442,000 | 384,000 – 512,000 |

## 3. Cost-of-Living and Station Adjustments

| Posting region | Base adjustment |
|---|---|
| Core Worlds (Coruscant, Corellia, Kuat) | 0% |
| Inner Rim | +4% |
| Mid Rim | +9% |
| Outer Rim | +12% |
| Unknown Regions / Wild Space | +18% plus hazard pay per Section 9 |

Adjustments are calculated against base salary and re-evaluated annually
on the personnel record's effective date.

## 4. Health and Medical Coverage

- **Imperial medical facilities:** 100% covered. Includes inpatient,
  outpatient, surgical, mental-health, and rehabilitation services.
- **Approved civilian providers:** 80% reimbursement up to a per-incident
  cap of 12,000 credits. Pre-authorization required for non-emergency
  expenditures exceeding 4,000 credits.
- **Dental and prosthetic:** Bundled with medical. Civilian-side
  reimbursement at 75%, capped at 4,000 credits per course of treatment.
- **Vision:** Covered at Imperial facilities; civilian reimbursement at
  60%, capped at 1,500 credits per standard year.
- **Cybernetic enhancement:** Covered only when medically necessary and
  certified by an Imperial medical board. Cosmetic cybernetic enhancement
  is not covered.

## 5. Family Benefits

- **Family transit allowance (Inner Rim and beyond):** 2,400 credits per
  dependent per standard year.
- **Dependent education subsidy:** 90% of tuition at Imperial Academy
  preparatory institutions, capped at 18,000 credits per dependent per
  standard year, for personnel with 3+ years of service.
- **Childcare credit:** 4,800 credits per standard year per dependent
  under 12, payable for personnel on Inner Rim or further postings.
- **Spousal relocation support:** One-time payment of 9,000 credits on
  cross-system reassignment exceeding 12 standard months in duration.

## 6. Retirement and Pension

The Imperial Service Pension Fund (ISPF) is a defined-contribution plan.

- **Imperial Service contribution:** 6.0% of base salary, deposited
  monthly.
- **Personnel contribution:** Up to 8.0% of base salary, pre-tax.
- **Match:** 50% match on the first 4% of personnel contribution.
- **Vesting:** Personnel contributions vest immediately. Imperial
  contributions vest at 25% per standard year, fully vested at 4 years.
- **Withdrawal age:** 55 standard years of age or 30 years of service,
  whichever is earlier.

## 7. Leave and Time Off

| Leave type | Accrual | Carry-forward cap |
|---|---|---|
| Compensated leave | 2.5 days / month (30 / year) | 30 days |
| Sick leave | 1.0 day / month | None |
| Bereavement leave | 5 days per event | N/A |
| Primary parental leave | 60 days per event | N/A |
| Secondary parental leave | 14 days per event | N/A |
| Sabbatical (10+ years service) | 30 days every 5 years | N/A |

## 8. Bonuses and Recognition

- **Service anniversaries:** 5-year increments. Bonus = 2 weeks of base
  pay at each milestone.
- **Performance bonus pool:** Up to 8% of base salary, awarded annually at
  Director discretion based on documented review.
- **Mission completion bonus:** 1,500–8,000 credits per qualifying
  operation, scaled by complexity and personal contribution.

## 9. Hazard Pay

Hazard pay is computed as a multiplier on base salary for the duration of
qualifying assignment.

- **Standard hazard (Outer Rim patrol):** +15%
- **Elevated hazard (active conflict zones):** +30%
- **Critical hazard (boarding actions, hyperspace anomaly response):** +60%

Hazard pay is taxable but does not count toward the pension contribution
base.
